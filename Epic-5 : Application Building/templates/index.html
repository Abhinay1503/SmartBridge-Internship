Predictive Meteorological Engine

Our machine learning models analyze 15 individual geological and meteorological factors (such as soil saturation, elevation indexes, drainage density, and multi-seasonal rainfall patterns) to calculate flood probability.

During the automated pipeline training phase, four distinct models are evaluated:

Decision Tree (Baseline)
K-Nearest Neighbors
Random Forest (Ensemble)
XGBoost (Target Model)

The best model is automatically selected and serialized to power the prediction panel. Our current best XGBoost model achieves an accuracy of approximately 96.55%.
